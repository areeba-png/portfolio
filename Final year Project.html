Project Explanation

Our Final Year Project titled "Geo-Spatial Data Analysis over Sindh Region" is a web-based platform that utilizes geo-spatial data and satellite imagery to track vegetation, non-vegetation land, and vegetation health across Sindh province.

Key Features

  • Calculates environmental indices like NDVI and LAI
  • Provides real-time vegetation analysis without technical skills
  • Allows users to upload GeoJSON files and choose timeframes
  • Delivers depth visualizations of vegetation health and land change
  • Serves farmers, researchers, and policymakers
